Year 4 History - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Roman Empire and Impact on Britain: Julius Caesar (55-54 BCE), Claudius invasion (CE 43), Hadrian's Wall, Roman roads, Roman towns (Bath, York), Roman army, Boudicea's rebellion, Roman withdrawal (CE 410), legacy (roads, language, buildings)
TOPIC 2: Ancient Greece: Athens and Sparta; democracy in Athens; gods and goddesses (Zeus, Athena, Poseidon); Olympics; Greek theatre; Alexander the Great; architecture (Parthenon); philosophy; Greek legacy
TOPIC 3: Historical Interpretation: Different accounts of same events; bias in sources; fact vs opinion; historians' interpretations may differ; evaluate sources critically
TOPIC 4: Chronology: Place events on timeline; understand century/decade; calculate time intervals; use BC/AD or BCE/CE correctly
