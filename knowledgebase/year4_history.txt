Year 4 History - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study Romans in Britain
- Learn about Anglo-Saxons
- Understand Viking invasions
- Compare historical periods
- Use historical sources

Chapters:

1, Roman Empire, Julius Caesar, Roman army, roads, baths, villas, Hadrians Wall
2, Anglo-Saxons, Saxons, settlements, kingdoms, Sutton Hoo, Anglo-Saxon culture
3, Vikings, longships, raids, Danelaw, Norse gods, Viking life
4, Historical Impact, legacy, influence, comparing periods, change and continuity
