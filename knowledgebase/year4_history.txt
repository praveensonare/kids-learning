Year 4 History - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study Romans in Britain
- Learn about Anglo-Saxons
- Understand Viking invasions
- Compare historical periods
- Use historical sources

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Roman Empire
description: Julius Caesar, Roman army, Roads, Baths

chapter no: 2
name: Anglo-Saxons
description: Saxons, Settlements, Kingdoms, Sutton Hoo

chapter no: 3
name: Vikings
description: Longships, Raids, Danelaw, Norse gods

chapter no: 4
name: Historical Impact
description: Legacy, Influence, Comparing periods, Change and continuity
