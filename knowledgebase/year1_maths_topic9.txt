Year 1 Maths - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 9: Time

Theory: We use clocks and calendars to measure and organize time.

Time Concepts:
- Days of the week: Monday to Sunday (7 days)
- Months of the year: January to December (12 months)
- Seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter

Telling Time:
- O'clock: minute hand on 12, hour hand shows hour
- Half past: minute hand on 6, hour hand halfway between hours
- Digital and analog clocks

Time Language:
- Before, after, next, yesterday, today, tomorrow
- Morning, afternoon, evening, night
- Earlier, later, quicker, slower

Worksheets:
1. Draw Clock Hands: Show 3 o'clock and 7 o'clock
2. Match Times: Connect analog clocks to digital times
3. Daily Routine: What time do you wake up? Eat breakfast?
4. Duration: Circle activities that take less than 1 minute
