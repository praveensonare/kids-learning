Year 4 English - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Reading - Fiction/Non-Fiction/Poetry: Themes, inference from actions/dialogue, predict from stated/implied details, summarize main ideas, identify how language/structure/presentation contribute to meaning
TOPIC 2: Writing Narrative: Detailed settings/characters, varied sentence structures (simple, compound, complex), paragraphs for cohesion, fronted adverbials, powerful vocabulary
TOPIC 3: Grammar: Determiners, pronouns, possessive pronouns, fronted adverbials with commas, noun phrases, prepositions, conjunctions, inverted commas for speech and quotes
TOPIC 4: Spelling: Prefixes (in-, im-, il-, ir-, sub-, inter-), suffixes (-ation, -ly, -ous), homophones (accept/except, whose/who's), apostrophes for possession/contraction
TOPIC 5: Poetry Analysis: Figurative language, verse structure, rhythm and rhyme, performance, write own poems using techniques learned
