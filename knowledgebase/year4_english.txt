Year 4 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read and discuss a range of texts
- Write with clear structure and detail
- Use advanced punctuation
- Expand vocabulary
- Understand grammar rules

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Advanced Reading
description: Analyzing, Evaluating, Comparing texts, Themes

chapter no: 2
name: Creative Writing
description: Descriptive writing, Settings, Characters, Suspense

chapter no: 3
name: Advanced Grammar
description: Fronted adverbials, Pronouns, Determiners, Clauses

chapter no: 4
name: Punctuation Mastery
description: Inverted commas, Apostrophes, Commas in lists, Direct speech
