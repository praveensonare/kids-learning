# Year 4 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 4

### Learning Objectives
- Read increasingly challenging texts
- Write for a range of purposes
- Use organizational and presentational devices
- Extend vocabulary and grammatical knowledge
- Discuss words and phrases that capture the reader's interest

### Key Topics

#### Reading
- Fiction, non-fiction, and poetry
- Predicting and summarizing
- Drawing inferences about characters
- Identifying main ideas
- Understanding themes and conventions

#### Writing
- Narrative composition
- Non-narrative forms
- Poetry and performance
- Using paragraphs effectively
- Expanded noun phrases
- Fronted adverbials

#### Grammar
- Determiners, pronouns, and prepositions
- Standard English forms
- Adverbs and adverbials
- Subordinating and coordinating conjunctions
- Plural and possessive apostrophes

#### Spelling
- Prefixes and suffixes
- Homophones and near-homophones
- Word families
- Statutory spelling words

### Sample Activities
- Guided reading discussions
- Extended writing projects
- Vocabulary development
- Grammar investigations
- Creative storytelling
