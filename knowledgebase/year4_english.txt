Year 4 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read and discuss a range of texts
- Write with clear structure and detail
- Use advanced punctuation
- Expand vocabulary
- Understand grammar rules

Chapters:

1, Advanced Reading, analyzing, evaluating, comparing texts, themes, language features
2, Creative Writing, descriptive writing, settings, characters, suspense, poetry
3, Advanced Grammar, fronted adverbials, pronouns, determiners, clauses, expanded noun phrases
4, Punctuation Mastery, inverted commas, apostrophes, commas in lists, direct speech
