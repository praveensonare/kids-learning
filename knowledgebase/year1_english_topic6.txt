Year 1 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 6: Story Writing

Theory: Stories have a clear structure with beginning, middle, and end. They include characters, settings, and events.

Story Structure:
- Beginning: Introduce characters and setting
- Middle: Problem or adventure happens
- End: Problem solved, story concludes

Story Elements:
- Who: Characters (give them names)
- Where: Setting (describe the place)
- When: Time (morning, long ago, etc.)
- What: Events (what happens)
- Feelings: How characters feel

Example Story Plan:
Beginning: "Once upon a time, there was a brave knight named Sir Tom."
Middle: "He went to rescue a dragon from a mean wizard."
End: "Sir Tom freed the dragon and they became friends."

Worksheets:
1. Story Plan: Draw and label beginning, middle, end
2. Character Description: Draw and describe your character
3. Story Starter: Continue "One day, I found a magic key..."
4. Rewrite: Retell a fairy tale in your own words
