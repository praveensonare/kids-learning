Year 6 History - Cambridge Primary Standards (Preparation for Secondary)

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Britain 1066-1485: Norman Conquest (1066); Battle of Hastings; Feudal system; Domesday Book; castles (motte-and-bailey, stone); Medieval life (lords, knights, peasants); Magna Carta (1215); Black Death (1348); Wars of the Roses; Henry Tudor
TOPIC 2: World War II (1939-1945): Causes (Treaty of Versailles, rise of Hitler); key events (Dunkirk, Battle of Britain, D-Day); Home Front (rationing, evacuation, Blitz); Holocaust; Churchill; VE Day; impact on Britain and world
TOPIC 3: Local Study: Significant turning point or event in local area; use range of sources; understand different interpretations; place in national context
TOPIC 4: Changes in Aspect of Life: Crime and punishment through ages, or leisure and entertainment, or transport, or homes, showing continuity and change from ancient times to present
TOPIC 5: Historical Enquiry: Frame historically valid questions; select and organize relevant information; understand how knowledge constructed from sources; evaluate sources for reliability
