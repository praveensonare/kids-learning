Year 2 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count in steps of 2, 3, 5, and 10
- Add and subtract two-digit numbers
- Learn multiplication and division
- Work with fractions
- Measure accurately

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Place Value to 100
description: Tens, Ones, Comparing, Ordering

chapter no: 2
name: Four Operations
description: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division

chapter no: 3
name: Fractions
description: Halves, Quarters, Thirds, Equal parts

chapter no: 4
name: Measurement
description: Length, Mass, Temperature, Capacity
