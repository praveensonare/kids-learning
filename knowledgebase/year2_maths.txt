# Year 2 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 2

### Learning Objectives
- Count, read and write numbers to 100
- Add and subtract two-digit numbers
- Multiply and divide using 2, 5 and 10 times tables
- Identify and describe properties of 2D and 3D shapes
- Use standard units of measurement

### Key Topics

#### Number and Place Value
- Numbers to 100
- Place value (tens and ones)
- Comparing and ordering numbers
- Odd and even numbers
- Counting in steps (2, 3, 5, 10)

#### Addition and Subtraction
- Mental strategies
- Column addition and subtraction
- Inverse operations
- Fact families
- Word problems and reasoning

#### Multiplication and Division
- 2, 5, and 10 times tables
- Multiplication as repeated addition
- Division as sharing and grouping
- Arrays and groups
- Commutativity

#### Fractions
- Halves, quarters, thirds
- Equivalent fractions (1/2 = 2/4)
- Finding fractions of amounts

#### Measurement
- Length (cm, m)
- Mass (g, kg)
- Capacity (ml, l)
- Temperature (°C)
- Time (hours, minutes, seconds)
- Money (£ and p)

#### Geometry
- Properties of 2D shapes (sides, vertices, symmetry)
- Properties of 3D shapes (faces, edges, vertices)
- Position and movement
- Turns and angles (quarter, half, three-quarter, full turn)

#### Statistics
- Tally charts
- Pictograms
- Block diagrams
- Interpreting data

### Activities
- Times table practice
- Measuring real objects
- Money problems (shopping scenarios)
- Shape investigations
- Data collection projects
