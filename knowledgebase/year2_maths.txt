Year 2 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count in steps of 2, 3, 5, and 10
- Add and subtract two-digit numbers
- Learn multiplication and division
- Work with fractions
- Measure accurately

Chapters:

1, Place Value to 100, tens, ones, comparing, ordering, number patterns, partitioning
2, Four Operations, addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, word problems
3, Fractions, halves, quarters, thirds, equal parts, fraction of amount
4, Measurement, length, mass, temperature, capacity, time, money
