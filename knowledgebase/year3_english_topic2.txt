Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 2: Writing - Narrative

Story Elements:
- Settings: describe time and place vividly
- Characters: develop personalities, feelings, motivations  
- Plot: clear structure with problem and resolution
- Dialogue: punctuated speech to show character

Techniques:
- Show don't tell: actions reveal feelings
- Varied sentence openers: -ly words, -ing words, time phrases
- Descriptive language: powerful verbs, precise adjectives
- Paragraphs: new speaker, new time, new place, new idea
