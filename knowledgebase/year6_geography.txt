Year 6 Geography - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study world regions in depth
- Understand economic geography
- Learn about migration
- Study environmental issues
- Use advanced map skills

Chapters:

1, Global Regions, Asia, Africa, latitude, longitude, time zones, hemispheres
2, Economic Geography, trade, industry, agriculture, tourism, economic development
3, Environmental Issues, deforestation, pollution, conservation, endangered species, sustainability
4, Migration and Settlement, population, migration, urban, rural, push factors, pull factors
