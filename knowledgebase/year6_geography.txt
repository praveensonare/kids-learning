Year 6 Geography - Cambridge Primary Standards (Preparation for Secondary)

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

1, Global Geography: All continents; major countries; lines of latitude/longitude; Equator, Tropics of Cancer/Capricorn, Arctic/Antarctic Circles; time zones; global climate patterns, Learn about global geography: all continents; major countries; lines of latitude/longitude; equator, tropics of cancer/capricorn, arctic/antarctic circles; time zones; global climate patterns
2, Physical Geography Processes: Mountains (fold, volcanic, fault-block); rivers (erosion, deposition, flood plains); coasts (erosion, weathering, deposition, longshore drift); earthquakes and volcanoes (plate tectonics), Learn about physical geography processes: mountains (fold, volcanic, fault-block); rivers (erosion, deposition, flood plains); coasts (erosion, weathering, deposition, longshore drift); earthquakes and volcanoes (plate tectonics)
3, Human Geography: Population distribution and density; urbanization; megacities; migration (push/pull factors); settlements; economic activities; trade links; resources (renewable, non-renewable), Learn about human geography: population distribution and density; urbanization; megacities; migration (push/pull factors); settlements; economic activities; trade links; resources (renewable, non-renewable)
4, Regional Study: In-depth study of region (e.g., Russia, Arctic, Middle East, Asia); physical features; climate; people and culture; economy; connections with other regions, Learn about regional study: in-depth study of region (e.g., russia, arctic, middle east, asia); physical features; climate; people and culture; economy; connections with other regions
5, Geographical Skills: OS maps; contour lines; six-figure grid references; scales and distances; fieldwork techniques; data presentation (graphs, GIS); aerial photos and satellite images, Learn about geographical skills: os maps; contour lines; six-figure grid references; scales and distances; fieldwork techniques; data presentation (graphs, gis); aerial photos and satellite images
6, Sustainability and Conservation: Climate change causes/effects; carbon footprint; renewable energy; sustainable cities; conservation efforts; individual actions, Learn about sustainability and conservation: climate change causes/effects; carbon footprint; renewable energy; sustainable cities; conservation efforts; individual actions
