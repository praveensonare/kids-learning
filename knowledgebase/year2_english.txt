Year 2 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read longer texts with fluency and expression
- Write for different purposes with correct grammar and punctuation
- Use expanded noun phrases and different sentence types
- Develop comprehension skills through inference
- Build vocabulary through wide reading
- Participate in discussions and presentations

Topics:

TOPIC 1: Advanced Phonics and Spelling
TOPIC 2: Reading Comprehension and Inference
TOPIC 3: Grammar - Sentence Types and Tenses
TOPIC 4: Expanded Noun Phrases
TOPIC 5: Story Writing with Structure
TOPIC 6: Non-Fiction Writing
TOPIC 7: Poetry and Performance
