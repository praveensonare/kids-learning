Year 2 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read accurately and fluently
- Write narratives and non-fiction
- Use correct punctuation consistently
- Spell common words correctly
- Develop vocabulary

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Reading Comprehension
description: Inference, Prediction, Retrieval, Vocabulary

chapter no: 2
name: Narrative Writing
description: Stories, Character, Setting, Plot

chapter no: 3
name: Punctuation and Grammar
description: Commas, Apostrophes, Questions, Statements

chapter no: 4
name: Spelling Patterns
description: Spelling rules, Homophones, Contractions, Common exception words
