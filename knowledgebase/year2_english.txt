Year 2 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read accurately and fluently
- Write narratives and non-fiction
- Use correct punctuation consistently
- Spell common words correctly
- Develop vocabulary

Chapters:

1, Reading Comprehension, inference, prediction, retrieval, vocabulary, summarizing
2, Narrative Writing, stories, character, setting, plot, past tense, description
3, Punctuation and Grammar, commas, apostrophes, questions, statements, commands, exclamations
4, Spelling Patterns, spelling rules, homophones, contractions, common exception words
