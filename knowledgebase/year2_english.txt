Year 2 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read longer texts with fluency and expression
- Write for different purposes with correct grammar and punctuation
- Use expanded noun phrases and different sentence types
- Develop comprehension skills through inference
- Build vocabulary through wide reading
- Participate in discussions and presentations

Topics:

1, Advanced Phonics and Spelling, Learn about advanced phonics and spelling
2, Reading Comprehension and Inference, Learn about reading comprehension and inference
3, Grammar - Sentence Types and Tenses, Learn about grammar - sentence types and tenses
4, Expanded Noun Phrases, Learn about expanded noun phrases
5, Story Writing with Structure, Learn about story writing with structure
6, Non-Fiction Writing, Learn about non-fiction writing
7, Poetry and Performance, Learn about poetry and performance
