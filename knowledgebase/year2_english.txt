# Year 2 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 2

### Learning Objectives
- Read age-appropriate books with fluency and expression
- Write narratives and non-fiction texts
- Use expanded noun phrases
- Apply spelling rules and patterns
- Understand different types of sentences

### Key Topics

#### Reading Comprehension
- Fiction and non-fiction texts
- Making predictions
- Inference and deduction
- Identifying main ideas
- Character analysis

#### Writing Skills
- Story structure (beginning, middle, end)
- Using adjectives and adverbs
- Past and present tense consistency
- Coordinating conjunctions (and, but, or, so)
- Subordinating conjunctions (when, if, that, because)

#### Grammar and Punctuation
- Noun phrases
- Statements, questions, exclamations, commands
- Commas in lists
- Apostrophes for contractions
- Capital letters for proper nouns

#### Spelling
- Common exception words
- Adding suffixes (-ing, -ed, -er, -est, -y)
- Homophones (there/their, to/too/two)
- Compound words
- Vowel digraphs and trigraphs

#### Poetry
- Rhyming patterns
- Performance poetry
- Creating simple poems

### Sample Texts
- Chapter books (Roald Dahl, Julia Donaldson)
- Non-fiction texts on various topics
- Poems by classic and modern poets
- Instructions and recipes

### Activities
- Guided reading with discussion
- Creative writing tasks
- Spelling investigations
- Drama and role-play
- Book reviews
