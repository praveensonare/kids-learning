Nursery Numeracy - EYFS Framework

Learning Objectives:
- Develop counting skills to 10
- Recognize numerals 0-10
- Compare quantities using language more/less
- Understand one-to-one correspondence
- Begin to explore simple patterns

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Numbers 1-5
description: Counting to 5, Number Recognition 1-5, One-to-One Correspondence, Comparing Quantities to 5

chapter no: 2
name: Numbers 6-10
description: Counting to 10, Number Recognition 6-10, Number Bonds to 5, Zero

chapter no: 3
name: Shapes and Space
description: Basic 2D Shapes, Basic 3D Shapes, Shape Properties, Sorting Shapes

chapter no: 4
name: Patterns and Positions
description: Simple Patterns, Repeating Patterns, Positional Language, Size Ordering
