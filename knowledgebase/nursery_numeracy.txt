# Nursery - Early Numeracy Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

### Learning Objectives
- Count reliably up to 10
- Recognize numerals 1-10
- Understand one-to-one correspondence
- Compare quantities (more/less/same)
- Recognize and name basic shapes

### Key Topics

#### Number Recognition
- Numbers 1-10
- Counting objects
- Number formation

#### Basic Shapes
- Circle, square, triangle, rectangle
- Shape properties
- Shapes in the environment

#### Patterns and Sorting
- Simple patterns (AB, ABB)
- Sorting by color, size, shape
- Matching activities

#### Measurement Concepts
- Big and small
- Long and short
- Heavy and light
- Full and empty

### Activities
- Counting songs and rhymes
- Building with blocks
- Shape sorting games
- Pattern making with objects
- Measuring and comparing activities
