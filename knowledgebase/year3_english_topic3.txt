Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 3: Grammar and Punctuation

- Present perfect: have/has + past participle (I have eaten)
- Conjunctions: when, before, after, while, because, if
- Adverbs: quickly, carefully, yesterday, soon
- Prepositions: above, below, beside, through, during
- Inverted commas: "speech marks" for dialogue
- Apostrophes: possession (girl's, girls')
- Paragraphs: group related sentences
