Nursery Science - Chapter 3: Weather Watch

TOPIC 1: Weather

Introduction:
Observing and talking about different types of weather.

Activities:
1. Daily weather observations and charts
2. Looking at the sky - clouds, sun, rain
3. Feeling the temperature - hot or cold day?
4. Weather dressing-up game
5. Creating weather symbols and charts

Key Vocabulary:
weather, sunny, rainy, cloudy, windy, hot, cold, wet, dry

Resources Needed:
- Weather chart
- Weather symbols
- Outdoor thermometer
- Weather clothing
- Weather books

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can describe today's weather? Knows appropriate clothing for weather? Uses weather words?

---

TOPIC 2: Seasons

Introduction:
Learning about the four seasons and their changes.

Activities:
1. Seasonal walks - observing changes
2. Making seasonal pictures (autumn leaves, spring flowers)
3. Talking about seasonal activities (summer beach, winter snow)
4. Seasonal food tasting
5. Creating a seasons display

Key Vocabulary:
spring, summer, autumn, winter, season, warm, cool, change, nature

Resources Needed:
- Seasonal pictures
- Natural materials
- Season books
- Art materials
- Calendar

Assessment Checkpoint:
Aware of seasonal changes? Can name current season? Notices nature changes?

---

TOPIC 3: Sun and Rain

Introduction:
Understanding the sun and rain and how they affect us.

Activities:
1. Shadow investigations on sunny days
2. Sun safety discussions - hats and sunscreen
3. Rain observations - puddles and raindrops
4. Making rainbows with water spray
5. Collecting rainwater experiments

Key Vocabulary:
sun, rain, shadow, puddle, rainbow, drops, umbrella, sunny day, rainy day

Resources Needed:
- Sun hats
- Umbrellas
- Containers for water
- Spray bottles
- Shadow materials

Assessment Checkpoint:
Knows sun is hot and provides light? Understands rain makes things wet? Basic sun safety?

---

TOPIC 4: Wind

Introduction:
Exploring wind and air movement.

Activities:
1. Feeling wind on face on windy days
2. Making wind catchers and wind socks
3. Blowing bubbles and watching them move
4. Using fans to create wind
5. Observing things moved by wind (flags, trees)

Key Vocabulary:
wind, windy, blow, air, breeze, flag, wave, move

Resources Needed:
- Windmills
- Streamers
- Bubbles
- Fans
- Windsocks

Assessment Checkpoint:
Knows wind is moving air? Can feel and observe wind effects? Uses wind vocabulary?
