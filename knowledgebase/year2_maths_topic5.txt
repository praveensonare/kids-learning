Year 2 Maths - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 5: Measurement

Theory: We use standard units to measure accurately and communicate measurements.

Length:
- Units: millimeters (mm), centimeters (cm), meters (m)
- Tools: ruler, tape measure, meter stick
- 1 meter = 100 centimeters
- Examples: pencil = 15 cm, door = 2 m

Mass/Weight:
- Units: grams (g), kilograms (kg)
- Tools: scales, balance
- 1 kilogram = 1000 grams
- Examples: apple = 100g, bag of sugar = 1kg

Capacity:
- Units: milliliters (ml), liters (l)
- Tools: measuring jug, beaker
- 1 liter = 1000 milliliters
- Examples: spoon = 5ml, bottle = 500ml, bucket = 5l
