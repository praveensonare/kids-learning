Year 2 Art & Design - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

1, Drawing Skills: Different pencils (H, HB, B); observational drawing with detail; patterns and textures; continuous line drawing; mark-making, Learn about drawing skills: different pencils (h, hb, b); observational drawing with detail; patterns and textures; continuous line drawing; mark-making
2, Painting Techniques: Warm colors (red, orange, yellow) vs cool colors (blue, green, purple); painting moods; thick and thin brushes; watercolor techniques; landscape painting, Learn about painting techniques: warm colors (red, orange, yellow) vs cool colors (blue, green, purple); painting moods; thick and thin brushes; watercolor techniques; landscape painting
3, Printing: Block printing with objects; patterns and repeats; potato/sponge printing; creating wrapping paper designs; symmetry in prints, Learn about printing: block printing with objects; patterns and repeats; potato/sponge printing; creating wrapping paper designs; symmetry in prints
4, Textiles: Weaving with paper/fabric strips; simple stitching; fabric collage; creating patterns on fabric, Learn about textiles: weaving with paper/fabric strips; simple stitching; fabric collage; creating patterns on fabric
5, Artist Studies: Henri Matisse (cut-outs, bright colors), Claude Monet (water lilies, Impressionism), Paul Klee (geometric patterns, colors), Learn about artist studies: henri matisse (cut-outs, bright colors), claude monet (water lilies, impressionism), paul klee (geometric patterns, colors)
