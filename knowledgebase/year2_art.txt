Year 2 Art - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Develop techniques in drawing, painting and sculpture
- Learn about great artists and designers
- Use sketch books to record observations
- Mix and use a range of colors

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Drawing and Mark Making
description: Sketching, Shading, Texture, Tone

chapter no: 2
name: Color Theory
description: Color wheel, Warm colors, Cool colors, Tints

chapter no: 3
name: Print Making
description: Printing, Patterns, Repeated patterns, Stamps

chapter no: 4
name: Famous Artists
description: Monet, Van Gogh, Art movements, Impressionism
