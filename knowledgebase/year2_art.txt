Year 2 Art & Design - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Drawing Skills: Different pencils (H, HB, B); observational drawing with detail; patterns and textures; continuous line drawing; mark-making
TOPIC 2: Painting Techniques: Warm colors (red, orange, yellow) vs cool colors (blue, green, purple); painting moods; thick and thin brushes; watercolor techniques; landscape painting
TOPIC 3: Printing: Block printing with objects; patterns and repeats; potato/sponge printing; creating wrapping paper designs; symmetry in prints
TOPIC 4: Textiles: Weaving with paper/fabric strips; simple stitching; fabric collage; creating patterns on fabric
TOPIC 5: Artist Studies: Henri Matisse (cut-outs, bright colors), Claude Monet (water lilies, Impressionism), Paul Klee (geometric patterns, colors)
