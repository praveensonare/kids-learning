Year 2 Art - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Develop techniques in drawing, painting and sculpture
- Learn about great artists and designers
- Use sketch books to record observations
- Mix and use a range of colors

Chapters:

1, Drawing and Mark Making, sketching, shading, texture, tone, detail, observation
2, Color Theory, color wheel, warm colors, cool colors, tints, shades
3, Print Making, printing, patterns, repeated patterns, stamps, textures
4, Famous Artists, Monet, Van Gogh, art movements, impressionism, style
