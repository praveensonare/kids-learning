Nursery Literacy - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Recognize and name all letters of the alphabet
- Understand that letters represent sounds
- Develop phonological awareness through rhymes and songs
- Handle books correctly and understand print concepts
- Begin to form recognizable letters
- Listen to and enjoy stories
- Develop speaking and listening skills

Chapters:

1, Alphabet Adventure, letters, uppercase, lowercase, recognition, sounds, phonics, alphabet song
2, Sound Explorers, phonics, initial sounds, rhyming, syllables, word families, sound games
3, Story Time Magic, comprehension, characters, sequence, prediction, retelling, story elements
4, Writing Readiness, pre-writing, fine motor, patterns, shapes, pencil grip, letter formation
