Nursery Literacy - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Recognize and name all letters of the alphabet
- Understand that letters represent sounds
- Develop phonological awareness through rhymes and songs
- Handle books correctly and understand print concepts
- Begin to form recognizable letters
- Listen to and enjoy stories
- Develop speaking and listening skills

Topics:

1, Alphabet Recognition, Learn to identify all 26 letters in uppercase and lowercase forms and understand letter names
2, Phonological Awareness and Sounds, Develop sound recognition through rhyming, syllables, and initial sounds in words
3, Print Awareness, Understand how books work including front cover, page turning, and reading direction
4, Story Comprehension, Listen to stories and understand characters, settings, and sequences of events
5, Pre-Writing Skills, Practice fine motor control through patterns, lines, and basic shapes needed for writing
6, Rhyming and Word Families, Recognize and create rhyming words and understand word patterns like -at, -an, -ig
7, Speaking and Listening, Develop communication skills through conversation, following instructions, and expressing ideas
