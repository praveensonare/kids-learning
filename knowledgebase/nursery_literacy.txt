# Nursery - Early Literacy Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

### Learning Objectives
- Recognize and identify letters of the alphabet
- Understand that letters represent sounds
- Begin to form letters correctly
- Develop phonological awareness
- Build vocabulary through stories and rhymes

### Key Topics

#### Letter Recognition
- Uppercase and lowercase letters
- Letter sounds (phonics)
- Letter formation and writing

#### Early Reading Skills
- Picture books and storytelling
- Understanding story sequence
- Identifying characters and settings

#### Speaking and Listening
- Express ideas clearly
- Listen to others attentively
- Follow simple instructions
- Participate in group discussions

### Activities
- Singing alphabet songs
- Tracing letters in sand/foam
- Reading picture books together
- Rhyming games and activities
- Show and tell sessions
