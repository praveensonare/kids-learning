Nursery Literacy - EYFS Framework

Learning Objectives:
- Develop phonological awareness
- Understand that print carries meaning
- Recognize letters and begin letter-sound correspondence
- Enjoy stories, songs and rhymes
- Begin mark-making and pre-writing skills

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Alphabet Fun
description: Letter Recognition A-M, Letter Recognition N-Z, Initial Sounds, Print Awareness

chapter no: 2
name: Fruits and Vegetables
description: Common Fruits, Common Vegetables, Where Food Comes From, Healthy Eating

chapter no: 3
name: Vehicles and Transport
description: Land Vehicles, Air and Water Vehicles, Vehicle Parts and Functions, Road Safety

chapter no: 4
name: Stories and Rhymes
description: Story Time, Nursery Rhymes, Story Sequencing, Role Play
