Year 5 Geography - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study North and South America
- Understand biomes
- Learn about climate change
- Study rivers in detail
- Understand human geography

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Americas
description: North America, South America, Countries, Physical features

chapter no: 2
name: Biomes and Climate
description: Rainforest, Desert, Tundra, Grassland

chapter no: 3
name: Rivers
description: River features, Erosion, Deposition, Flood plains

chapter no: 4
name: Climate Change
description: Global warming, Greenhouse gases, Sustainability, Renewable energy
