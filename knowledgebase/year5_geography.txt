Year 5 Geography - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives: Locate world countries focusing on N&S America, identify human/physical features, understand biomes, use digital/computer mapping

Key Topics:
TOPIC 1: North America in Detail: USA states and cities; Canada provinces; Mexico; physical features (Grand Canyon, Great Lakes, Rocky Mountains); climate zones; economic activities; population distribution
TOPIC 2: South America: Countries and capitals; Amazon Rainforest (extent, importance, deforestation); Andes Mountains; climate zones; indigenous peoples; economic activities (farming, mining)
TOPIC 3: Biomes: Rainforests (tropical, temperate); deserts (hot, cold); tundra; savanna; characteristics; plants and animals adapted; climate; threats to biomes
TOPIC 4: Environmental Issues: Deforestation causes and effects; climate change; conservation; sustainable development; human impact on environment
TOPIC 5: Economic Geography: Trade; imports and exports; fair trade; how goods get from production to consumers; economic activities (primary, secondary, tertiary)
Worksheets: Biome comparison charts, rainforest layers diagram, trade route mapping, environmental impact studies, data interpretation
