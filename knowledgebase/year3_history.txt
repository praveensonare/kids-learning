Year 3 History - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study Stone Age to Iron Age
- Learn about Ancient Egypt
- Understand chronology
- Investigate historical sources
- Compare past and present

Chapters:

1, Stone Age Britain, hunter gatherers, stone tools, Skara Brae, prehistoric, cave art
2, Bronze and Iron Ages, bronze, iron, hillforts, farming, technology
3, Ancient Egypt, pharaohs, pyramids, mummies, Nile, hieroglyphics, civilization
4, Using Historical Evidence, sources, artifacts, archaeology, timelines, chronology
