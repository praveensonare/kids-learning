Year 3 History - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives: Develop chronological understanding, build overview of British/local history, note connections/contrasts, understand methods of historical enquiry

Key Topics:
TOPIC 1: Stone Age to Iron Age Britain: Paleolithic, Mesolithic, Neolithic periods; hunter-gatherers; farming begins; Stonehenge; Bronze Age; Iron Age hill forts; changes in daily life over time
TOPIC 2: Ancient Egypt: Pharaohs (Tutankhamun, Cleopatra); pyramids and tombs; River Nile importance; hieroglyphics; mummification; gods and goddesses (Ra, Anubis, Isis); daily life; social hierarchy
TOPIC 3: Local History Study: Investigate aspect of local area from long ago; use buildings, sites, artifacts as evidence; compare past and present; significant events or people in local history
TOPIC 4: Historical Skills: Timelines; primary vs secondary sources; ask and answer questions using sources; understand BCE/CE dates; make comparisons between periods
Worksheets: Timeline creation, artifact analysis, hieroglyphic writing, comparing ancient and modern life, source interpretation
