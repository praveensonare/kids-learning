Year 3 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Ask relevant questions and set up simple tests
- Make systematic observations and measurements
- Gather, record, and present data
- Use evidence to answer questions
- Identify differences and similarities
- Use scientific vocabulary

Topics:

1, Plants, Learn about plants
2, Animals Including Humans, Learn about animals including humans
3, Rocks and Soils, Learn about rocks and soils
4, Light and Shadows, Learn about light and shadows
5, Forces and Magnets, Learn about forces and magnets
