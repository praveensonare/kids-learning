Year 3 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Compare and group rocks
- Understand nutrition and skeletons
- Explore forces and magnets
- Investigate light and shadows
- Study plants

Chapters:

1, Rocks and Soils, igneous, sedimentary, metamorphic, fossils, soil formation, permeability
2, Animals and Humans, nutrition, skeleton, muscles, bones, joints, diet
3, Forces and Magnets, push, pull, friction, magnetic, poles, attract, repel
4, Light and Shadow, light sources, reflection, mirrors, shadows, sun safety
