Year 6 Maths - Cambridge Primary Standards (Preparation for Secondary)

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Place Value to 10,000,000: Read, write, order, compare; powers of 10; negative numbers in context
TOPIC 2: Four Operations: Multi-step problems; long multiplication/division; BIDMAS (Brackets, Indices, Division/Multiplication, Addition/Subtraction); mental calculations with large numbers
TOPIC 3: Fractions/Decimals/Percentages: Simplify fractions; common factors/multiples; add/subtract fractions with different denominators; multiply/divide fractions; 4 decimal places; percentages of amounts; percentage increase/decrease
TOPIC 4: Ratio and Proportion: Ratio notation; solve problems; scale factors; similar shapes; unequal sharing
TOPIC 5: Algebra: Use simple formulae; generate sequences; express missing number problems algebraically; find pairs of numbers for equations with two unknowns; enumerate possibilities
TOPIC 6: Measurement: Convert between standard units; area of triangles/parallelograms; volume of cubes/cuboids
TOPIC 7: Geometry: Classify shapes; angles in polygons; draw 2D shapes; coordinates (4 quadrants); translate/reflect shapes; names of parts of circle
TOPIC 8: Statistics: Pie charts, line graphs, mean average; interpret and solve problems
