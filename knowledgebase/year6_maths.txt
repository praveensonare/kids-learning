Year 6 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Work with numbers to 10000000
- Master all four operations
- Work fluently with fractions, decimals and percentages
- Solve complex problems
- Apply mathematical reasoning

Chapters:

1, Number and Place Value, ten million, negative numbers, rounding, estimation, calculations
2, Calculation Mastery, four operations, order of operations, mental strategies, problem solving
3, FDP and Ratio, fractions, decimals, percentages, ratio, proportion, algebra
4, Geometry and Statistics, circles, area, volume, mean, graphs, pie charts
