Year 6 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Work with numbers to 10000000
- Master all four operations
- Work fluently with fractions, decimals and percentages
- Solve complex problems
- Apply mathematical reasoning

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Number and Place Value
description: Ten million, Negative numbers, Rounding, Estimation

chapter no: 2
name: Calculation Mastery
description: Four operations, Order of operations, Mental strategies, Problem solving

chapter no: 3
name: FDP and Ratio
description: Fractions, Decimals, Percentages, Ratio

chapter no: 4
name: Geometry and Statistics
description: Circles, Area, Volume, Mean
