# Year 6 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 6

### Learning Objectives
- Read, write, order and compare numbers up to 10,000,000
- Perform all four operations with confidence
- Work fluently with fractions, decimals and percentages
- Solve complex multi-step problems
- Apply mathematical reasoning

### Key Topics

#### Number
- Numbers to 10,000,000
- Negative numbers in context
- Order of operations (BODMAS/BIDMAS)
- Rounding and estimation

#### Four Operations
- Long multiplication and division
- Mental calculation strategies
- Multi-step problems
- Algebraic reasoning

#### Fractions, Decimals and Percentages
- All four operations with fractions
- Decimal calculations
- Percentage of amounts
- Ratio and proportion
- Algebra basics

#### Measurement
- Converting and calculating with metric and imperial units
- Area, perimeter and volume
- Circles (radius, diameter)
- Problem solving with measure

#### Geometry
- Properties of 2D and 3D shapes
- Classifying shapes
- Finding unknown angles
- Coordinates in four quadrants
- Transformations (translation, reflection, rotation)

#### Statistics
- Pie charts
- Mean averages
- Interpreting and constructing graphs
- Probability

### Activities
- SATs preparation
- Real-world problem solving
- Mathematical investigations
- Transition to secondary mathematics
