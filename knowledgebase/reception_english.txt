Reception English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read simple CVC (consonant-vowel-consonant) words using phonics
- Write simple sentences with capital letters and full stops
- Develop comprehension skills through listening and discussion
- Build vocabulary through stories and conversations
- Form letters correctly with appropriate size and spacing
- Recognize high-frequency words by sight

Topics:

1, Phonics (Phase 2-4), Learn about phonics (phase 2-4)
2, High-Frequency Words (Tricky Words), Learn about high-frequency words (tricky words)
3, Reading CVC and Simple Sentences, Learn about reading cvc and simple sentences
4, Writing - Letter Formation, Learn about writing - letter formation
5, Sentence Writing, Learn about sentence writing
6, Story Comprehension, Learn about story comprehension
7, Vocabulary and Language, Learn about vocabulary and language
