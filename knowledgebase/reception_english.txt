Reception English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read and write phonetically plausible words
- Read common exception words
- Write simple sentences
- Develop comprehension skills
- Understand story structure

Chapters:

1, Phonics Foundations, phonics, letter sounds, blending, segmenting, CVC words, digraphs
2, Reading Adventures, reading, decoding, sight words, fluency, common exception words
3, Writing Journey, sentence writing, capital letters, full stops, finger spaces, handwriting
4, Story Comprehension, understanding, prediction, inference, retelling, story structure
