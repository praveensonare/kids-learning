Reception English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read and write phonetically plausible words
- Read common exception words
- Write simple sentences
- Develop comprehension skills
- Understand story structure

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Phonics Foundations
description: Phonics, Letter sounds, Blending, Segmenting

chapter no: 2
name: Reading Adventures
description: Reading, Decoding, Sight words, Fluency

chapter no: 3
name: Writing Journey
description: Sentence writing, Capital letters, Full stops, Finger spaces

chapter no: 4
name: Story Comprehension
description: Understanding, Prediction, Inference, Retelling
