# Reception - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) / Primary Stage 1

### Learning Objectives
- Develop phonemic awareness
- Read simple sentences
- Write recognizable letters and words
- Comprehend simple stories
- Express ideas in simple sentences

### Key Topics

#### Phonics
- Phase 2-4 phonics sounds
- Blending sounds to read words
- Segmenting words to spell
- Tricky words (the, no, go, I)
- CVC words (cat, dog, sun)

#### Reading
- Reading simple sentences
- Picture books and stories
- Predicting what might happen
- Story sequencing
- Character recognition

#### Writing
- Letter formation (upper and lowercase)
- Writing simple words
- Writing simple sentences
- Using capital letters and full stops
- Finger spaces between words

#### Speaking and Listening
- Expressing thoughts clearly
- Following multi-step instructions
- Engaging in story time
- Asking questions
- Sharing news and experiences

### Sample Activities
- Daily phonics sessions
- Shared reading
- Independent writing tasks
- Story retelling
- Role-play and drama
