Reception English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 3: Reading CVC and Simple Sentences

Theory: Reading involves decoding words by blending sounds and using picture clues. Fluent reading comes with practice.

Examples:
Simple Sentences:
- "The cat sat."
- "I can run."
- "Dad is big."
- "Look at the dog!"

Reading Strategies:
- Sound out each word
- Look at the picture for clues
- Re-read if it doesn't make sense
- Think: does that sound right?

Worksheets:
1. Read and Match: Read the sentence "The cat is fat" and draw a picture
2. Yes or No: "Can a pig fly?" (No)
3. Sentence Completion: "I can ___ fast." (run/hop/swim)
4. Comprehension: Read "Sam has a red ball. He plays in the park." Questions: Who? What? Where?
