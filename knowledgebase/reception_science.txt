Reception Science - Exploring the World Around Us

Learning Objectives:
- Observe and describe the natural and physical world
- Ask questions and test simple ideas
- Identify and name common plants and animals
- Understand the properties of different materials
- Recognize seasonal changes and weather patterns
- Explore how things work through investigation
- Develop scientific vocabulary and observational skills

Topics:

1, Living Things - Animals and Their Habitats, Learn about living things - animals and their habitats
2, Plants and Growth, Learn about plants and growth
3, Human Body and Health, Learn about human body and health
4, Materials, Properties, and Changes, Learn about materials, properties, and changes
5, Seasons, Weather, and Climate, Learn about seasons, weather, and climate
6, Light and Dark, Learn about light and dark
7, Sound and Hearing, Learn about sound and hearing
