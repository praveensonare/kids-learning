Reception Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Observe and describe the natural world
- Understand life cycles
- Explore materials and their properties
- Investigate forces and movement
- Learn about seasonal changes

Chapters:

1, Living Things, animals, plants, growth, life cycle, baby animals, seeds
2, Material Detectives, materials, wood, plastic, metal, fabric, properties, uses
3, Seasons and Weather, spring, summer, autumn, winter, weather patterns, seasonal changes
4, Push and Pull, forces, push, pull, movement, speed, direction
