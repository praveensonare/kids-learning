Year 5 History - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Anglo-Saxons and Scots (CE 410-1066): Migration after Romans left; kingdoms (Northumbria, Mercia, Wessex); King Alfred the Great; Anglo-Saxon culture (Beowulf, Sutton Hoo); Christianity spreads; Viking raids and settlements; Edward the Confessor
TOPIC 2: Vikings (CE 793-1066): Viking invasions; Lindisfarne raid (793); Danelaw; Viking culture (longships, sagas, gods); trade and exploration; King Cnut; Harald Hardrada; Battle of Stamford Bridge
TOPIC 3: Early Islamic Civilization (CE 600-1600): Prophet Muhammad; spread of Islam; Baghdad House of Wisdom; achievements in mathematics (algebra, algorithms), medicine, astronomy, architecture; compare with European Dark Ages
TOPIC 4: Historical Study Non-European: Maya civilization, Benin Kingdom, or Shang Dynasty; achievements, daily life, legacy
