Year 5 History - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Study Ancient Greece
- Learn about Greek legacy
- Understand democracy
- Study Greek mythology
- Compare ancient civilizations

Chapters:

1, Ancient Greek Civilization, Athens, Sparta, city-states, Greek society, daily life
2, Greek Democracy, democracy, government, citizenship, voting, assembly
3, Greek Culture, Olympics, theater, philosophy, architecture, art
4, Greek Legacy, influence, modern world, alphabet, mathematics, science
