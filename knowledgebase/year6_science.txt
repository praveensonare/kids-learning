Year 6 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Classify living things
- Understand human circulatory system
- Study evolution and inheritance
- Learn about light
- Explore electricity in depth

Chapters:

1, Classification, micro-organisms, Carl Linnaeus, kingdoms, classification keys
2, Circulation and Health, heart, blood vessels, circulatory system, healthy lifestyle, diet, exercise
3, Evolution and Inheritance, adaptation, evolution, inheritance, fossils, Charles Darwin
4, Light and Electricity, light travels, shadows, reflection, circuits, symbols, voltage
