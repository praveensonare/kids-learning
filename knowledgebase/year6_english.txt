Year 6 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Master reading comprehension
- Write confidently for different audiences
- Apply advanced grammar accurately
- Use sophisticated vocabulary
- Evaluate texts critically

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Critical Reading
description: Evaluation, Comparing texts, Implicit meaning, Bias

chapter no: 2
name: Advanced Writing
description: Formal writing, Informal writing, Voice, Audience

chapter no: 3
name: Grammar Mastery
description: Subjunctive, Passive, Perfect tense, Colons

chapter no: 4
name: SATs Preparation
description: Exam techniques, Comprehension strategies, Spelling rules, Revision
