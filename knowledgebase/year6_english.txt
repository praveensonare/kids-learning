Year 6 English - Cambridge Primary Standards (Preparation for Secondary)

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

1, Critical Reading: Infer characters' feelings/thoughts/motives from actions; predict from details; identify how language/structure/presentation contribute to meaning; distinguish between statements of fact/opinion; evaluate effectiveness; compare across books, Learn about critical reading: infer characters' feelings/thoughts/motives from actions; predict from details; identify how language/structure/presentation contribute to meaning; distinguish between statements of fact/opinion; evaluate effectiveness; compare across books
2, Sophisticated Writing: Viewpoint, formal/informal tone, atmosphere, dialogue advances action, précis, description, narrative hooks, cohesive paragraphs, selecting grammar/vocabulary for effect, Learn about sophisticated writing: viewpoint, formal/informal tone, atmosphere, dialogue advances action, précis, description, narrative hooks, cohesive paragraphs, selecting grammar/vocabulary for effect
3, Advanced Grammar: Subjunctive (If I were, I wish I were), passive voice, synonyms/antonyms for effect, modal verbs, semi-colons/colons/dashes, hyphens, subject/object, active/passive sentences, Learn about advanced grammar: subjunctive (if i were, i wish i were), passive voice, synonyms/antonyms for effect, modal verbs, semi-colons/colons/dashes, hyphens, subject/object, active/passive sentences
4, Spelling Mastery: Challenging words, words from statutory list, word families, word origins, prefixes/suffixes, silent letters, homophones, Learn about spelling mastery: challenging words, words from statutory list, word families, word origins, prefixes/suffixes, silent letters, homophones
5, Formal Presentations: Debates, performances, formal speeches, Shakespeare, classic literature analysis, Learn about formal presentations: debates, performances, formal speeches, shakespeare, classic literature analysis
