# Year 6 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 6

### Learning Objectives
- Read, comprehend and analyze complex texts
- Write effectively for a variety of purposes
- Use advanced grammatical structures
- Develop sophisticated vocabulary
- Prepare for secondary education

### Key Topics

#### Reading
- Wide range of genres
- Literary analysis
- Identifying themes and conventions
- Comparing writers' use of language
- Making predictions and inferences
- Evaluating texts critically

#### Writing
- Narrative writing with structure
- Non-fiction across genres
- Formal and informal writing
- Adapting writing for audience and purpose
- Using cohesion within and across paragraphs
- Advanced sentence structures

#### Grammar
- Subjunctive forms
- Formal and informal structures
- Active and passive voice
- Synonyms and antonyms
- Cohesive devices
- Advanced punctuation

#### Spelling
- Word origins and etymology
- Complex prefixes and suffixes
- Challenging vocabulary
- Subject-specific terminology

### Sample Activities
- Extended reading comprehension
- Essay writing
- Debate and discussion
- Research and presentation
- Preparation for secondary school
