Year 6 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Master reading comprehension
- Write confidently for different audiences
- Apply advanced grammar accurately
- Use sophisticated vocabulary
- Evaluate texts critically

Chapters:

1, Critical Reading, evaluation, comparing texts, implicit meaning, bias, perspective
2, Advanced Writing, formal writing, informal writing, voice, audience, purpose
3, Grammar Mastery, subjunctive, passive, perfect tense, colons, semicolons, dashes
4, SATs Preparation, exam techniques, comprehension strategies, spelling rules, revision
