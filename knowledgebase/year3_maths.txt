# Year 3 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 3

### Learning Objectives
- Count, read and write numbers to 1000
- Add and subtract three-digit numbers
- Recall multiplication and division facts for 3, 4 and 8 tables
- Understand fractions (tenths, unit and non-unit fractions)
- Measure using standard units accurately

### Key Topics

#### Number and Place Value
- Numbers to 1000
- Hundreds, tens and ones
- Comparing and ordering
- Rounding to nearest 10 and 100
- Roman numerals I to XII

#### Addition and Subtraction
- Mental calculations with 3-digit numbers
- Column methods for 3-digit numbers
- Estimation and checking
- Problem solving with multiple steps

#### Multiplication and Division
- 3, 4 and 8 times tables
- Multiplying and dividing by 10 and 100
- Formal written methods
- Scaling problems
- Correspondence problems

#### Fractions
- Unit fractions (1/2, 1/3, 1/4, 1/5, 1/10)
- Non-unit fractions
- Equivalent fractions
- Comparing and ordering fractions
- Adding and subtracting fractions with same denominator

#### Measurement
- Perimeter of 2D shapes
- Mass, length, capacity (reading scales)
- Time (24-hour clock, duration)
- Money (pounds and pence, giving change)

#### Geometry
- Parallel and perpendicular lines
- 2D and 3D shape properties
- Right angles in shapes
- Horizontal and vertical lines

#### Statistics
- Bar charts
- Pictograms with scales
- Tables and data interpretation

### Activities
- Mental arithmetic tests
- Problem-solving investigations
- Practical measuring tasks
- Fraction wall activities
- Real-life maths projects
