Year 3 Maths - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count, read, write numbers to 1000
- Add and subtract 3-digit numbers
- Know 3, 4, 8 times tables
- Understand tenths and fractions
- Measure perimeter
- Tell time using 12-hour and 24-hour clocks
- Interpret bar charts and tables

Key Topics:

TOPIC 1: Place Value to 1000
- Hundreds, tens, ones: 456 = 400 + 50 + 6
- Comparing and ordering 3-digit numbers
- Rounding to nearest 10 or 100
- Counting in 50s and 100s
- Roman numerals: I, V, X, L, C (1, 5, 10, 50, 100)

TOPIC 2: Addition and Subtraction
Mental strategies:
- Add/subtract multiples of 10 and 100
- Partition numbers
- Inverse operations

Written methods:
- Column addition with carrying (478 + 356)
- Column subtraction with exchanging (523 - 187)
- Check with inverse operation

TOPIC 3: Multiplication and Division
Times tables: 2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 10
- Related division facts
- Multiply 2-digits by 1-digit: 23 × 4
- Divide 2-digits by 1-digit: 48 ÷ 4

Written methods:
- Short multiplication
- Division with remainders

TOPIC 4: Fractions
Unit and non-unit fractions:
- Understand 1/3, 1/4, 1/5, 1/6, 1/8, 1/10
- Add and subtract fractions with same denominator: 2/5 + 1/5 = 3/5
- Find fractions of amounts: 1/4 of 20 = 5
- Equivalent fractions: 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8
- Compare and order fractions
- Tenths as decimals: 0.1, 0.2, 0.3

TOPIC 5: Measurement
Length and perimeter:
- mm, cm, m, km
- Perimeter: add all sides

Mass and capacity:
- g, kg (1kg = 1000g)
- ml, l (1l = 1000ml)

Money:
- Add and subtract amounts
- Give change from £5, £10

TOPIC 6: Geometry
2D shapes:
- Properties of polygons
- Right angles in shapes
- Parallel and perpendicular lines

3D shapes:
- Identify faces, edges, vertices
- Nets of cubes and cuboids

TOPIC 7: Time
- 12-hour and 24-hour clocks
- AM and PM
- Duration: calculate time intervals
- Roman numerals on clocks

TOPIC 8: Statistics
- Bar charts with intervals
- Pictograms with symbols representing multiple units
- Tables and two-way tables
- Interpret and present data

Worksheets and Activities:
1. Place value grids to 1000
2. Column addition and subtraction
3. Times tables practice (3, 4, 8)
4. Fraction walls and equivalents
5. Calculate perimeter of shapes
6. Time problems with duration
7. Create and interpret bar charts
8. Real-life money problems
