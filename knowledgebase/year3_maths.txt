Year 3 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Understand place value to 1000
- Master times tables
- Work with fractions
- Measure and calculate
- Understand angles and shapes

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Place Value
description: Hundreds, Tens, Ones, Numbers to 1000

chapter no: 2
name: Multiplication and Division
description: Times tables, 3x, 4x, 8x tables

chapter no: 3
name: Fractions
description: Unit fractions, Non-unit fractions, Equivalent fractions, Comparing fractions

chapter no: 4
name: Geometry and Measurement
description: Angles, Right angles, Perpendicular, Parallel
