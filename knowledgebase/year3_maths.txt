Year 3 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Understand place value to 1000
- Master times tables
- Work with fractions
- Measure and calculate
- Understand angles and shapes

Chapters:

1, Place Value, hundreds, tens, ones, numbers to 1000, comparing, rounding
2, Multiplication and Division, times tables, 3x, 4x, 8x tables, mental strategies, written methods
3, Fractions, unit fractions, non-unit fractions, equivalent fractions, comparing fractions
4, Geometry and Measurement, angles, right angles, perpendicular, parallel, perimeter
