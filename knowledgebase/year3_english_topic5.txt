Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 5: Poetry

Forms:
- Haiku: 5-7-5 syllables
- Kennings: two-word descriptions (wave-rider = ship)  
- Free verse: no rhyme scheme
- Performance poetry: rhythm and repetition

Devices:
- Similes and metaphors
- Personification: giving human qualities to objects
- Alliteration and onomatopoeia
- Imagery: painting pictures with words

Worksheets and Activities:
1. Character profiles with evidence from text
2. Write dialogue between characters
3. Create story mountain plans
4. Use adverbs and conjunctions correctly
5. Spell words with prefixes and suffixes
6. Analyze and write poetry
7. Edit and improve writing
8. Present stories to an audience
