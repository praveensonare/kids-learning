Year 4 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Classify living things
- Understand digestive system
- Study states of matter
- Explore electricity
- Learn about sound

Chapters:

1, Living Things, classification, vertebrates, invertebrates, habitats, food chains
2, Digestion, digestive system, teeth, food chains, producers, consumers
3, States of Matter, solids, liquids, gases, changes of state, evaporation, condensation
4, Electricity and Sound, circuits, conductors, insulators, vibrations, pitch, volume
