Year 4 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Classify living things
- Understand digestive system
- Study states of matter
- Explore electricity
- Learn about sound

chapter no: 2

chapter no: 3
name: States of Matter
description: Solids, Liquids, Gases, Changes of state

chapter no: 4
name: Electricity and Sound
description: Circuits, Conductors, Insulators, Vibrations
