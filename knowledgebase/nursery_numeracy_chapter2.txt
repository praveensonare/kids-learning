Nursery Numeracy - Chapter 2: Numbers 6-10

TOPIC 1: Counting to 10

Introduction:
Extending counting skills to 10.

Activities:
1. Counting fingers on both hands
2. Counting larger groups of objects
3. Number songs to 10
4. Counting movements (jumps, claps)
5. Counting backwards from 10

Key Vocabulary:
six, seven, eight, nine, ten, count on, count back

Resources Needed:
- Ten frames
- Counting objects
- Number line 0-10

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can count reliably to 10? Beginning to count backwards?

---

TOPIC 2: Number Recognition 6-10

Introduction:
Learning to recognize numerals 6-10.

Activities:
1. Number treasure hunts
2. Ordering number cards 1-10
3. Number matching to quantities
4. Writing numbers in sand/paint
5. Number games and puzzles

Key Vocabulary:
numeral, digit, order, sequence, before, after

Resources Needed:
- Number cards 1-10
- Number puzzles
- Writing materials

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can recognize most numerals to 10? Can order some numbers?

---

TOPIC 3: Number Bonds to 5

Introduction:
Exploring ways to make numbers to 5.

Activities:
1. Part-whole games (5 = 3 and 2)
2. Ten frame activities
3. Finding pairs that make 5
4. Finger combinations for numbers
5. Real-life splitting activities

Key Vocabulary:
makes, total, altogether, and, split, part

Resources Needed:
- Ten frames
- Counters
- Part-whole cards

Assessment Checkpoint:
Beginning to see numbers as composed of smaller numbers?

---

TOPIC 4: Zero

Introduction:
Understanding zero means nothing/none.

Activities:
1. Countdown activities to zero
2. Empty set activities
3. Zero in everyday life (no cookies left)
4. Number line including zero
5. Subtraction stories to zero

Key Vocabulary:
zero, none, nothing, empty, all gone

Resources Needed:
- Number line with 0
- Empty containers
- Countdown props

Assessment Checkpoint:
Understands zero represents nothing? Can identify numeral 0?
