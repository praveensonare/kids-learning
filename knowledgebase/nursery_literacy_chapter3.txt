Nursery Literacy - Chapter 3: Vehicles and Transport

TOPIC 1: Land Vehicles

Introduction:
Exploring vehicles that travel on roads.

Activities:
1. Learning about car, bus, truck, bicycle, train, motorcycle
2. Vehicle sound games
3. Building vehicle models with blocks
4. Vehicle sorting by size, number of wheels
5. Creating roads and traffic scenarios

Key Vocabulary:
car, bus, truck, bicycle, train, motorcycle, wheels, road, driver, fast, slow

Resources Needed:
- Toy vehicles
- Vehicle books
- Building blocks
- Road play mats

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can name 4-6 common vehicles? Understands they go on roads?

---

TOPIC 2: Air and Water Vehicles

Introduction:
Learning about vehicles that fly or float.

Activities:
1. Exploring airplane, helicopter, boat, ship concepts
2. Making paper airplanes
3. Water play with floating toy boats
4. Learning vehicle sounds (plane engine, boat horn)
5. Sky vs water vehicle sorting

Key Vocabulary:
airplane, helicopter, boat, ship, fly, float, sky, water, pilot, captain

Resources Needed:
- Toy planes and boats
- Water table
- Pictures of vehicles

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can identify vehicles that fly vs float? Names 2-3 air/water vehicles?

---

TOPIC 3: Vehicle Parts and Functions

Introduction:
Understanding how vehicles work and their parts.

Activities:
1. Identifying wheels, windows, doors, engines
2. Counting wheels on different vehicles
3. Learning vehicle purposes (ambulance helps sick people)
4. Safety discussions (seat belts, helmets)
5. Building vehicles from junk materials

Key Vocabulary:
wheels, engine, steering wheel, window, door, seat belt, helmet, safe

Resources Needed:
- Vehicle models
- Construction materials
- Safety equipment props

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can identify basic vehicle parts? Understanding of vehicle purposes?

---

TOPIC 4: Road Safety

Introduction:
Basic road safety awareness.

Activities:
1. Learning about traffic lights (red-stop, green-go)
2. Pedestrian crossing practice
3. Looking left and right before crossing
4. Role-play being safe pedestrians
5. Making traffic light crafts

Key Vocabulary:
stop, go, wait, safe, careful, look, listen, cross, traffic light, zebra crossing

Resources Needed:
- Traffic light props
- Road crossing mat
- Hi-vis vests

Assessment Checkpoint:
Shows awareness of basic road safety? Knows stop and go?
