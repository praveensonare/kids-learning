Year 2 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Ask questions and perform simple tests
- Observe closely and gather data
- Identify and classify living things
- Understand uses of everyday materials
- Describe how animals obtain food
- Explain importance of exercise, diet, and hygiene
- Observe seasonal changes over the year

Topics:

1, Living Things and Their Habitats, Learn about living things and their habitats
2, Plants, Learn about plants
3, Animals Including Humans, Learn about animals including humans
4, Uses of Everyday Materials, Learn about uses of everyday materials
5, Seasonal Changes, Learn about seasonal changes
