Year 2 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Understand animal habitats and food chains
- Explore plant growth
- Investigate materials and their uses
- Learn about healthy living
- Discover forces and motion

Chapters:

1, Living Things and Habitats, habitats, microhabitats, food chains, living, dead, never alive
2, Plants, seeds, bulbs, growth, water, light, temperature, germination
3, Uses of Materials, suitability, properties, changing shape, squashing, bending, twisting
4, Healthy Living, exercise, nutrition, hygiene, growth, basic needs
