Year 4 Geography - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Locate world countries
- Study European geography
- Understand earthquakes and volcanoes
- Learn about water cycle
- Study economic activity

Chapters:

chapter no: 2

chapter no: 3
name: Water Cycle
description: Evaporation, Condensation, Precipitation, Collection

chapter no: 4
name: Trade and Resources
description: Natural resources, Trade, Import, Export
