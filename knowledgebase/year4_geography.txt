Year 4 Geography - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Locate world countries
- Study European geography
- Understand earthquakes and volcanoes
- Learn about water cycle
- Study economic activity

Chapters:

1, Europe and Beyond, continents, countries, capital cities, European Union, flags
2, Mountains and Volcanoes, volcanoes, earthquakes, tectonic plates, Ring of Fire, mountain ranges
3, Water Cycle, evaporation, condensation, precipitation, collection, rivers
4, Trade and Resources, natural resources, trade, import, export, fair trade
