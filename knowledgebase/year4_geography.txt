Year 4 Geography - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Europe in Detail: Countries, capitals, major cities; mountain ranges (Alps, Pyrenees, Carpathians); rivers (Danube, Rhine, Rhone); climate patterns; land use; compare regions
TOPIC 2: North and South America: Countries and capitals; physical features (Andes, Amazon, Rockies, Mississippi); climate zones (rainforests, deserts, tundra); time zones
TOPIC 3: Water Cycle: Evaporation, condensation, precipitation, collection; importance of water; rivers (source, tributary, meander, mouth); flooding; water conservation
TOPIC 4: Map Skills Advanced: Eight compass points (N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW); six-figure grid references; contour lines show height; latitude and longitude; symbols and keys
TOPIC 5: Settlements: Types (hamlet, village, town, city); why settle there (water, trade routes, defense); land use; impact on environment
