Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read a range of fiction, non-fiction, and poetry
- Use dictionaries to check meanings
- Write narratives with detailed settings and characters
- Use conjunctions, adverbs, and prepositions
- Organize paragraphs around a theme
- Present ideas orally with expression

Topics:

TOPIC 1: Reading Comprehension
TOPIC 2: Writing - Narrative
TOPIC 3: Grammar and Punctuation
TOPIC 4: Spelling and Vocabulary
TOPIC 5: Poetry
