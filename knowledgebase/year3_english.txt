Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read a wide range of books
- Write structured narratives and non-fiction
- Use paragraphs to organize ideas
- Apply spelling rules
- Understand word classes

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Reading for Meaning
description: Comprehension, Inference, Authors intent, Themes

chapter no: 2
name: Writing Stories
description: Narrative structure, Paragraphs, Dialogue, Description

chapter no: 3
name: Grammar and Punctuation
description: Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives, Adverbs

chapter no: 4
name: Non-Fiction Writing
description: Information texts, Instructions, Explanations, Reports
