# Year 3 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 3

### Learning Objectives
- Read a range of fiction and non-fiction texts
- Write for different purposes and audiences
- Use organizational and presentational devices
- Extend sentence structure with conjunctions
- Increase knowledge of vocabulary and spelling patterns

### Key Topics

#### Reading
- Chapter books and novels
- Poetry appreciation
- Non-fiction texts (reports, explanations)
- Inference and deduction skills
- Identifying themes and conventions

#### Writing
- Narrative writing (plots, settings, characters)
- Non-narrative forms (letters, reports, instructions)
- Poetry composition
- Using paragraphs to organize ideas
- Fronted adverbials
- Direct speech with inverted commas

#### Grammar
- Word families and prefixes
- Expressing time, place and cause using conjunctions, adverbs and prepositions
- Present perfect form of verbs
- Expanded noun phrases with modifying adjectives

#### Punctuation
- Inverted commas for direct speech
- Commas after fronted adverbials
- Apostrophes for possession
- Exclamation marks and question marks

#### Spelling
- Prefixes (un-, dis-, mis-, re-, pre-)
- Suffixes (-ly, -ous, -tion, -ssion, -cian)
- Homophones
- Word families

### Sample Texts
- Classic children's literature
- Contemporary fiction
- Information texts
- Playscripts
- Poetry collections

### Activities
- Book clubs and reading groups
- Creative writing workshops
- Drama and performances
- Vocabulary building games
- Spelling investigations
