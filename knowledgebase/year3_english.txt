Year 3 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Read a wide range of books
- Write structured narratives and non-fiction
- Use paragraphs to organize ideas
- Apply spelling rules
- Understand word classes

Chapters:

1, Reading for Meaning, comprehension, inference, authors intent, themes, literary language
2, Writing Stories, narrative structure, paragraphs, dialogue, description, character development
3, Grammar and Punctuation, nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions
4, Non-Fiction Writing, information texts, instructions, explanations, reports, organization
