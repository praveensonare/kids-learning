# Year 1 - Science Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Science Stage 1

### Learning Objectives
- Identify and name common plants and animals
- Describe basic structures of common flowering plants
- Identify and name parts of the human body
- Distinguish between materials
- Observe seasonal changes

### Key Topics

#### Plants
- Common wild and garden plants
- Deciduous and evergreen trees
- Basic structure (roots, stem, leaves, flowers)
- Plant growth requirements

#### Animals Including Humans
- Common animals (fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds, mammals)
- Carnivores, herbivores and omnivores
- Parts of the human body
- Senses and their functions

#### Everyday Materials
- Wood, plastic, glass, metal, water, rock
- Properties (hard/soft, stretchy/stiff, shiny/dull, rough/smooth)
- Comparing and grouping materials
- Uses of materials

#### Seasonal Changes
- Observing changes across the four seasons
- Weather patterns
- Day length variations
- How seasons affect trees and animals

### Working Scientifically
- Asking simple questions
- Observing closely
- Performing simple tests
- Identifying and classifying
- Recording findings

### Activities
- Nature walks and observations
- Growing plants from seeds
- Sorting materials by properties
- Seasonal diaries
- Animal habitat studies
