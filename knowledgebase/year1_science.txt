Year 1 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Classify common animals
- Identify plants and their parts
- Distinguish materials by properties
- Understand seasonal changes
- Explore light and sound

Chapters:

1, Animals Including Humans, animal groups, mammals, birds, fish, reptiles, amphibians, body parts
2, Plants, plant parts, leaves, flowers, roots, stem, trees, wild plants
3, Everyday Materials, wood, plastic, glass, metal, water, rock, properties
4, Seasonal Changes, four seasons, weather, day length, temperature, observations
