Year 1 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Classify common animals
- Identify plants and their parts
- Distinguish materials by properties
- Understand seasonal changes
- Explore light and sound

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Animals Including Humans
description: Animal groups, Mammals, Birds, Fish

chapter no: 2
name: Plants
description: Plant parts, Leaves, Flowers, Roots

chapter no: 3
name: Everyday Materials
description: Wood, Plastic, Glass, Metal

chapter no: 4
name: Seasonal Changes
description: Four seasons, Weather, Day length, Temperature
