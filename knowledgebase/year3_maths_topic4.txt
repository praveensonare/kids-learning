Year 3 Maths - Cambridge Primary Curriculum
TOPIC 4: Fractions

Unit and non-unit fractions:
- Understand 1/3, 1/4, 1/5, 1/6, 1/8, 1/10
- Add and subtract fractions with same denominator: 2/5 + 1/5 = 3/5
- Find fractions of amounts: 1/4 of 20 = 5
- Equivalent fractions: 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8
- Compare and order fractions
- Tenths as decimals: 0.1, 0.2, 0.3
