# Year 5 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 5

### Learning Objectives
- Read and discuss a wide range of texts
- Write effectively for different purposes and audiences
- Use expanded grammatical knowledge
- Develop advanced vocabulary
- Participate in discussions about books

### Key Topics

#### Reading
- Classic and contemporary fiction
- Non-fiction texts
- Poetry analysis
- Comparing texts
- Making inferences
- Identifying author techniques

#### Writing
- Extended narrative writing
- Non-fiction genres
- Persuasive writing
- Descriptive writing
- Using cohesive devices
- Varying sentence structure

#### Grammar
- Relative clauses
- Modal verbs
- Adverbs of time and place
- Parenthesis
- Passive voice

#### Spelling
- Silent letters
- Word origins
- Prefixes and suffixes
- Homophones
- Complex word patterns

### Sample Activities
- Literature circles
- Research projects
- Debates and presentations
- Creative writing workshops
- Grammar and punctuation exercises
