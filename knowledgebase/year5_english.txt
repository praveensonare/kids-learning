Year 5 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Analyze complex texts
- Write for different purposes
- Master advanced grammar
- Use figurative language
- Develop critical reading

Chapters:

1, Literary Analysis, metaphor, simile, personification, analyzing language, authors purpose
2, Persuasive Writing, persuasion, argument, evidence, rhetorical questions, balanced arguments
3, Complex Grammar, relative clauses, modal verbs, passive voice, subjunctive, cohesion
4, Poetry and Performance, poetry forms, rhythm, meter, performance, interpretation
