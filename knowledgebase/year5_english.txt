Year 5 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Analyze complex texts
- Write for different purposes
- Master advanced grammar
- Use figurative language
- Develop critical reading

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Literary Analysis
description: Metaphor, Simile, Personification, Analyzing language

chapter no: 2
name: Persuasive Writing
description: Persuasion, Argument, Evidence, Rhetorical questions

chapter no: 3
name: Complex Grammar
description: Relative clauses, Modal verbs, Passive voice, Subjunctive

chapter no: 4
name: Poetry and Performance
description: Poetry forms, Rhythm, Meter, Performance
