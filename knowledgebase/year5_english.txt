Year 5 English - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Comprehension: Recommend books, make comparisons within/across books, themes and conventions, meaning from context, summarize from multiple paragraphs, distinguish fact/opinion, retrieve/record/present information from non-fiction
TOPIC 2: Writing Purposes: Narratives (viewpoints, atmosphere, dialogue), persuasive (arguments, evidence), explanations (cause/effect), reports (formal language)
TOPIC 3: Advanced Grammar: Modal verbs (might, should, will, must), relative clauses (who, which, that, where, when, whose), brackets/dashes/commas for parenthesis, adverbs, cohesion within/across paragraphs
TOPIC 4: Punctuation: Commas to clarify meaning, brackets/dashes for parenthesis, inverted commas and punctuation for direct speech
TOPIC 5: Spelling: Prefixes/suffixes (auto-, bi-, -cious/-tious, -cial/-tial), silent letters (knight, solemn), word origins (Greek/Latin roots)
