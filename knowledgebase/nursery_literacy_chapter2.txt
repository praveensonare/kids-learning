Nursery Literacy - Chapter 2: Fruits and Vegetables

TOPIC 1: Common Fruits

Introduction:
Learning about everyday fruits through sensory exploration.

Activities:
1. Fruit tasting sessions - apple, banana, orange, grapes, strawberry
2. Sorting fruits by color, size, shape
3. Fruit printing art activities
4. Learning fruit names and initial sounds
5. Healthy eating discussions

Key Vocabulary:
apple, banana, orange, grapes, strawberry, fruit, healthy, sweet, juicy

Resources Needed:
- Real fruits
- Plastic fruit models
- Picture cards
- Fruit books

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can name 3-5 common fruits? Understands fruits are healthy foods?

---

TOPIC 2: Common Vegetables

Introduction:
Exploring vegetables and their importance.

Activities:
1. Vegetable tasting - carrot, cucumber, tomato, potato, broccoli
2. Growing vegetables from seeds
3. Vegetable printing and art
4. Sorting by color - green vegetables, orange vegetables
5. Cooking simple vegetable snacks

Key Vocabulary:
carrot, cucumber, tomato, potato, broccoli, vegetable, grow, healthy, crunchy

Resources Needed:
- Real vegetables
- Growing kits
- Vegetable books
- Picture cards

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can name 3-5 vegetables? Shows willingness to try vegetables?

---

TOPIC 3: Where Food Comes From

Introduction:
Understanding that food grows and comes from different places.

Activities:
1. Learning about farms and gardens
2. Sorting: grows on trees vs grows in ground
3. Planting seeds and watching them grow
4. Visiting a farm or market (virtual or real)
5. Creating a classroom vegetable patch

Key Vocabulary:
grow, plant, seed, soil, water, farm, garden, tree, ground

Resources Needed:
- Growing kits
- Farm books
- Pictures of farms and gardens

Assessment Checkpoint:
Basic understanding that food comes from plants/farms?

---

TOPIC 4: Healthy Eating

Introduction:
Learning about eating fruits and vegetables for health.

Activities:
1. Creating healthy plate collages
2. Sorting healthy vs treat foods
3. Rainbow of fruits and vegetables activity
4. Making fruit kebabs or veggie sticks
5. Role-play grocery shopping for healthy foods

Key Vocabulary:
healthy, good for you, strong, grow, energy, rainbow, variety

Resources Needed:
- Food picture cards
- Play kitchen
- Shopping basket

Assessment Checkpoint:
Can identify some healthy foods? Beginning to understand healthy choices?
