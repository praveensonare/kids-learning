Year 1 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Apply phonic knowledge to decode words
- Read common exception words
- Write sentences with proper punctuation
- Use suffixes and prefixes
- Develop reading comprehension

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Advanced Phonics
description: Phase 5 phonics, Alternative spellings, Split digraphs, Trigraphs

chapter no: 2
name: Reading Skills
description: Fluency, Expression, Comprehension, Prediction

chapter no: 3
name: Sentence Construction
description: Sentences, Capital letters, Full stops, Question marks

chapter no: 4
name: Word Building
description: Prefixes, Suffixes, -ed, -ing
