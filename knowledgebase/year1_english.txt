Year 1 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Apply phonic knowledge to decode words
- Read common exception words
- Write sentences with proper punctuation
- Use suffixes and prefixes
- Develop reading comprehension

Chapters:

1, Advanced Phonics, Phase 5 phonics, alternative spellings, split digraphs, trigraphs, decoding
2, Reading Skills, fluency, expression, comprehension, prediction, inference
3, Sentence Construction, sentences, capital letters, full stops, question marks, exclamation marks
4, Word Building, prefixes, suffixes, -ed, -ing, -s, plural, word families
