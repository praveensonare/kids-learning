# Year 1 - English Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary English Stage 1

### Learning Objectives
- Read simple sentences with fluency
- Write simple sentences with correct punctuation
- Use phonics to decode unfamiliar words
- Spell common high-frequency words
- Understand story structure

### Key Topics

#### Phonics and Reading
- Phase 3-5 phonics sounds
- CVC, CVCC, CCVC words
- Blending and segmenting
- Common exception words
- Reading comprehension

#### Writing Skills
- Sentence structure (capital letters, full stops)
- Finger spaces between words
- Simple punctuation (! ?)
- Story writing basics
- Descriptive words (adjectives)

#### Grammar
- Nouns and verbs
- Singular and plural
- Simple tenses (present/past)
- Use of 'and' to join sentences

#### Speaking and Listening
- Clear expression of ideas
- Retelling stories
- Asking and answering questions
- Poetry recitation

### Sample Texts
- Traditional tales (Three Little Pigs, Goldilocks)
- Simple chapter books
- Poems and rhymes
- Information texts

### Activities
- Daily phonics practice
- Guided reading sessions
- Creative story writing
- Spelling tests
- Show and tell presentations
