Year 1 English - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Apply phonic knowledge to read and spell words accurately
- Read common exception words fluently
- Write sentences with correct punctuation and grammar
- Understand and retell stories with proper sequence
- Develop vocabulary through reading and discussion
- Participate confidently in speaking and listening activities

Topics:

1, Phonics - Phase 5 and Beyond, Learn about phonics - phase 5 and beyond
2, Common Exception Words, Learn about common exception words
3, Reading Comprehension, Learn about reading comprehension
4, Sentence Writing, Learn about sentence writing
5, Grammar Basics, Learn about grammar basics
6, Story Writing, Learn about story writing
7, Poetry and Rhyme, Learn about poetry and rhyme
