Year 5 Science - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Living Things/Life Cycles: Life cycles (mammal, amphibian, insect, bird); reproduction in plants (sexual, asexual); animal reproduction; Jane Goodall, David Attenborough
TOPIC 2: Animals/Humans: Changes through puberty, gestation periods, life expectancy
TOPIC 3: Properties/Changes of Materials: Compare properties, dissolving, separating mixtures (filtering, sieving, evaporating), reversible/irreversible changes (burning, rusting, mixing), new materials formed
TOPIC 4: Earth and Space: Earth, Sun, Moon (spherical bodies); Earth's rotation (day/night); Moon orbits Earth; Earth orbits Sun; planets in Solar System; Sun at center
TOPIC 5: Forces: Gravity, air resistance, water resistance, friction; mechanisms (levers, pulleys, gears) allow smaller force to have greater effect
