Year 5 Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Understand properties and changes of materials
- Study Earth and space
- Learn about forces
- Explore life cycles
- Investigate reversible changes

Chapters:

1, Materials and Properties, dissolving, separating mixtures, reversible changes, irreversible changes
2, Earth and Space, solar system, planets, Earth rotation, day and night, seasons
3, Forces, gravity, air resistance, water resistance, friction, mechanisms, levers
4, Life Cycles, reproduction, mammals, amphibians, insects, birds, plants
