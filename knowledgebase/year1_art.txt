Year 1 Art - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Use a range of materials creatively
- Develop drawing and painting techniques
- Explore color mixing
- Learn about famous artists
- Create sculptures

Chapters:

1, Drawing Skills, lines, shapes, patterns, observation, pencil, crayon
2, Painting Techniques, brushwork, color mixing, primary colors, secondary colors, textures
3, Sculpture and 3D, modeling, clay, construction, 3D forms, joining
4, Artists and Inspiration, famous artists, art styles, observing art, responding to art
