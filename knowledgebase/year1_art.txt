Year 1 Art & Design - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

1, Drawing: Pencils, crayons, pastels; lines (thick, thin, straight, curved, zigzag); shapes (circles, squares, triangles); observational drawing (fruits, toys, faces); shading techniques, Learn about drawing: pencils, crayons, pastels; lines (thick, thin, straight, curved, zigzag); shapes (circles, squares, triangles); observational drawing (fruits, toys, faces); shading techniques
2, Painting: Primary colors (red, yellow, blue); mixing to make secondary (orange, green, purple); brush techniques (dabbing, stroking, swirling); painting textures and patterns, Learn about painting: primary colors (red, yellow, blue); mixing to make secondary (orange, green, purple); brush techniques (dabbing, stroking, swirling); painting textures and patterns
3, Collage: Cutting, tearing, arranging paper; textures (smooth, rough, shiny, bumpy); creating pictures from shapes; tissue paper overlapping for color mixing, Learn about collage: cutting, tearing, arranging paper; textures (smooth, rough, shiny, bumpy); creating pictures from shapes; tissue paper overlapping for color mixing
4, 3D/Sculpture: Playdough, clay modeling; pinching, rolling, flattening techniques; creating simple models (animals, pots); joining pieces together, Learn about 3d/sculpture: playdough, clay modeling; pinching, rolling, flattening techniques; creating simple models (animals, pots); joining pieces together
5, Famous Artists: Andy Warhol (pop art bright colors), Vincent van Gogh (Sunflowers, swirls), Wassily Kandinsky (abstract shapes and colors), Learn about famous artists: andy warhol (pop art bright colors), vincent van gogh (sunflowers, swirls), wassily kandinsky (abstract shapes and colors)
