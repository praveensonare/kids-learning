Year 1 Art & Design - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives: Use drawing, painting, sculpture; develop techniques with color, pattern, texture, line, shape, form, space; learn about range of artists

Key Topics:
TOPIC 1: Drawing: Pencils, crayons, pastels; lines (thick, thin, straight, curved, zigzag); shapes (circles, squares, triangles); observational drawing (fruits, toys, faces); shading techniques
TOPIC 2: Painting: Primary colors (red, yellow, blue); mixing to make secondary (orange, green, purple); brush techniques (dabbing, stroking, swirling); painting textures and patterns
TOPIC 3: Collage: Cutting, tearing, arranging paper; textures (smooth, rough, shiny, bumpy); creating pictures from shapes; tissue paper overlapping for color mixing
TOPIC 4: 3D/Sculpture: Playdough, clay modeling; pinching, rolling, flattening techniques; creating simple models (animals, pots); joining pieces together
TOPIC 5: Famous Artists: Andy Warhol (pop art bright colors), Vincent van Gogh (Sunflowers, swirls), Wassily Kandinsky (abstract shapes and colors)
Worksheets: Self-portraits, color mixing charts, texture rubbings, shape pictures, artist study comparisons
