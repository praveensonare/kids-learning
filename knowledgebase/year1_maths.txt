Year 1 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count to 100 and beyond
- Add and subtract within 20
- Understand place value
- Measure and compare
- Work with fractions

Chapters:

1, Place Value, tens, ones, numbers to 100, comparing, ordering, number line
2, Addition and Subtraction, adding, subtracting, within 20, number bonds, word problems
3, Multiplication Intro, groups, arrays, doubling, halving, equal groups
4, Fractions and Measurement, half, quarter, length, weight, capacity, time
