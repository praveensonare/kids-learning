Year 1 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count to 100 and beyond
- Add and subtract within 20
- Understand place value
- Measure and compare
- Work with fractions

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Place Value
description: Tens, Ones, Numbers to 100, Comparing

chapter no: 2
name: Addition and Subtraction
description: Adding, Subtracting, Within 20, Number bonds

chapter no: 3
name: Multiplication Intro
description: Groups, Arrays, Doubling, Halving

chapter no: 4
name: Fractions and Measurement
description: Half, Quarter, Length, Weight
