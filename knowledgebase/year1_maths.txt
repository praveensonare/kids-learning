# Year 1 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 1

### Learning Objectives
- Count, read and write numbers to 100
- Add and subtract within 20
- Understand place value (tens and ones)
- Recognize and name 2D and 3D shapes
- Tell time to the hour and half hour

### Key Topics

#### Number and Place Value
- Numbers 0-100
- Counting forwards and backwards
- One more, one less
- Tens and ones (place value)
- Ordering and comparing numbers

#### Addition and Subtraction
- Number bonds to 10 and 20
- Addition facts within 20
- Subtraction facts within 20
- Missing number problems
- Word problems

#### Multiplication and Division (Introduction)
- Counting in 2s, 5s, 10s
- Equal groups
- Sharing and grouping
- Arrays

#### Measurement
- Length (longer/shorter, measuring with cubes)
- Weight (heavier/lighter)
- Capacity (full/empty, more/less)
- Time (days of week, months, o'clock, half past)

#### Geometry
- 2D shapes (circle, triangle, square, rectangle, pentagon, hexagon)
- 3D shapes (cube, cuboid, sphere, cylinder, cone)
- Position and direction (left/right, forwards/backwards)
- Patterns and sequences

#### Fractions
- Halves and quarters of shapes
- Halves and quarters of quantities

### Activities
- Mental maths warm-ups
- Practical measuring activities
- Shape hunts
- Number line games
- Money problems (1p, 2p, 5p, 10p coins)
