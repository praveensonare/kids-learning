Year 3 Geography - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Use maps and atlases
- Understand physical geography
- Compare locations
- Learn about different climates
- Study settlements

Chapters:

1, Maps and Directions, compass, north, south, east, west, symbols, keys
2, Rivers and Mountains, rivers, mountains, valleys, source, mouth, water cycle
3, Climate Zones, equator, tropics, poles, climate, temperature, rainfall
4, Settlements, village, town, city, land use, human geography
