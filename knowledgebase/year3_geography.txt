Year 3 Geography - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

1, UK Geography: Four countries (England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland); capital cities; major cities; mountains (Ben Nevis, Scafell Pike, Snowdon); rivers (Thames, Severn); coastal features, Learn about uk geography: four countries (england, scotland, wales, northern ireland); capital cities; major cities; mountains (ben nevis, scafell pike, snowdon); rivers (thames, severn); coastal features

5, Climate Zones: Equator, Tropics, Polar Regions; characteristics of hot/temperate/cold climates; how climate affects life, Learn about climate zones: equator, tropics, polar regions; characteristics of hot/temperate/cold climates; how climate affects life
