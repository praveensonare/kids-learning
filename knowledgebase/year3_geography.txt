Year 3 Geography - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives: Locate world's countries, identify physical/human features, understand climate zones, use maps/atlases/globes/digital mapping

Key Topics:
TOPIC 1: UK Geography: Four countries (England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland); capital cities; major cities; mountains (Ben Nevis, Scafell Pike, Snowdon); rivers (Thames, Severn); coastal features
TOPIC 2: Europe: Countries and capitals; physical features (Alps, Mediterranean Sea, Rhine River); climate differences; compare UK to European country
TOPIC 3: Volcanoes and Earthquakes: What causes them (tectonic plates); Ring of Fire; famous eruptions (Pompeii, Mount Vesuvius); earthquake safety; living near volcanoes
TOPIC 4: Map Skills: Four compass directions (N,S,E,W); map keys/legends; scale; grid references; atlas use; digital mapping
TOPIC 5: Climate Zones: Equator, Tropics, Polar Regions; characteristics of hot/temperate/cold climates; how climate affects life
Worksheets: UK map labeling, European country research, volcano diagrams, compass direction activities, climate zone comparison
