Nursery Science - Discovery and Exploration

Learning Objectives:
- Explore the natural world through observation and investigation
- Develop curiosity about living things, materials, and physical phenomena
- Learn to ask questions and make predictions
- Use all five senses to investigate objects and materials
- Understand basic concepts about animals, plants, and the environment
- Develop scientific vocabulary through hands-on experiences

Topics:

1, Living Things - Animals, Learn about living things - animals
2, Living Things - Plants, Learn about living things - plants
3, Our Bodies and Senses, Learn about our bodies and senses
4, Materials and Their Properties, Learn about materials and their properties
5, Weather and Seasons, Learn about weather and seasons
6, Day and Night, Learn about day and night
7, Forces and Movement, Learn about forces and movement
