Nursery Science - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Explore the natural world through observation
- Use senses to discover and investigate
- Understand basic needs of living things
- Observe changes in materials and objects
- Develop curiosity about how things work

Chapters:

1, My Five Senses, sight, hearing, touch, taste, smell, senses, observation
2, Plants and Animals, living things, plants, animals, growth, needs, habitats
3, Weather Watch, weather, seasons, sun, rain, wind, temperature, clouds
4, Materials Around Us, materials, properties, hard, soft, rough, smooth, textures
