# Nursery - Discovery (Science) Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

### Learning Objectives
- Explore the natural world around them
- Make observations and describe what they see
- Understand basic concepts of living things
- Develop curiosity about nature
- Learn through hands-on exploration

### Key Topics

#### Living Things
- Animals and their babies
- Plants and how they grow
- Caring for living things
- Habitats and homes

#### Materials
- Different textures (soft, hard, rough, smooth)
- Floating and sinking
- Exploring natural materials
- Everyday materials

#### Weather and Seasons
- Types of weather (sunny, rainy, windy, snowy)
- Seasonal changes
- Appropriate clothing for weather
- Simple weather observations

#### My Body
- Parts of the body
- Five senses (sight, hearing, touch, taste, smell)
- Healthy habits
- Movement and exercise

### Activities
- Nature walks and observations
- Planting seeds and watching them grow
- Water play and experiments
- Sensory exploration activities
- Weather charts
