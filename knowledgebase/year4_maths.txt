Year 4 Maths - Cambridge Primary Standards

Learning Objectives:


Topics:

TOPIC 1: Place Value to 10,000: Thousands, hundreds, tens, ones; compare and order; round to nearest 10, 100, 1000; Roman numerals to 100 (I, V, X, L, C)
TOPIC 2: Four Operations: Add/subtract 4-digits (column method); multiply 3-digits by 1-digit; divide 3-digits by 1-digit; mental strategies; inverse operations
TOPIC 3: Times Tables: All tables to 12×12; factor pairs; multiplication facts; related division facts; multiply by 10 and 100
TOPIC 4: Fractions/Decimals: Count in fractions; add/subtract with same denominator; recognize equivalent fractions; decimals (tenths and hundredths); compare decimals; money as decimals (£4.56)
TOPIC 5: Measurement: Convert units (km-m, m-cm, kg-g, l-ml); perimeter of rectangles; area by counting squares; 12-hour and 24-hour time
TOPIC 6: Geometry: Classify shapes by properties; angles (acute, obtuse, right); lines of symmetry; position and direction; translations; coordinates (first quadrant)
TOPIC 7: Statistics: Interpret/construct bar charts, time graphs, pictograms; discrete and continuous data
