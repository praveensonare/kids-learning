Year 4 Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Work with place value to 10000
- Master multiplication and division
- Understand decimals
- Calculate area and perimeter
- Work with coordinates

Chapters:

1, Place Value to 10000, thousands, hundreds, tens, ones, rounding, roman numerals
2, Times Tables and Division, 6x, 7x, 9x, 11x, 12x tables, factor pairs, division facts
3, Fractions and Decimals, equivalent fractions, decimals, tenths, hundredths, fraction problems
4, Area and Perimeter, area, perimeter, rectangles, rectilinear shapes, coordinates
