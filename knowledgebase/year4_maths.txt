# Year 4 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 4

### Learning Objectives
- Count, order and compare numbers beyond 1000
- Add and subtract numbers with up to 4 digits
- Recall multiplication and division facts to 12 × 12
- Recognize and write decimal equivalents of fractions
- Solve problems involving measure and money

### Key Topics

#### Number and Place Value
- Roman numerals to 100
- Rounding to nearest 10, 100, 1000
- Counting in multiples
- Negative numbers

#### Addition and Subtraction
- Mental and written methods
- 4-digit numbers
- Estimation and checking

#### Multiplication and Division
- Times tables to 12 × 12
- Mental methods
- Formal written methods
- Factor pairs

#### Fractions and Decimals
- Equivalent fractions
- Adding and subtracting fractions
- Decimal notation
- Rounding decimals

#### Measurement
- Converting units
- Perimeter and area
- Money calculations
- Time (12 and 24-hour)

#### Geometry
- Acute, obtuse, right angles
- 2D and 3D properties
- Symmetry and reflection
- Coordinates

### Activities
- Problem-solving tasks
- Real-world applications
- Mathematical investigations
- Mental arithmetic
