# Year 5 - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Primary Mathematics Stage 5

### Learning Objectives
- Read, write, order and compare numbers to 1,000,000
- Add and subtract whole numbers with more than 4 digits
- Multiply and divide numbers mentally and using formal methods
- Use fractions, decimals and percentages
- Solve multi-step problems

### Key Topics

#### Number
- Numbers to 1,000,000
- Roman numerals to 1000
- Negative numbers
- Prime numbers, square numbers, cube numbers

#### Four Operations
- Multi-digit calculations
- Order of operations
- Mental calculation strategies
- Problem solving

#### Fractions, Decimals and Percentages
- Comparing and ordering fractions
- Mixed numbers and improper fractions
- Decimals to 3 places
- Percentages as fractions and decimals
- Solving percentage problems

#### Measurement
- Converting metric units
- Volume and capacity
- Area and perimeter
- Time calculations

#### Geometry
- Properties of shapes
- Angles (acute, obtuse, reflex)
- Reflection and translation
- 3D shapes and nets

#### Statistics
- Tables and timetables
- Line graphs
- Interpreting data

### Activities
- Complex problem solving
- Real-life mathematics
- Investigations
- Data handling projects
