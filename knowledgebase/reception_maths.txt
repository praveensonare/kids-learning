Reception Maths - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count reliably to 20 and beyond
- Recognize, order, and write numbers 0-20
- Understand addition and subtraction within 10
- Recognize and describe 2D and 3D shapes
- Compare and measure using non-standard units
- Understand time concepts and solve simple problems

Topics:

1, Counting and Number Recognition (0-20), Learn about counting and number recognition (0-20)
2, One More, One Less, Learn about one more, one less
3, Comparing Numbers, Learn about comparing numbers
4, Number Bonds to 10, Learn about number bonds to 10
5, Addition Within 10, Learn about addition within 10
6, Subtraction Within 10, Learn about subtraction within 10
7, 2D and 3D Shapes, Learn about 2d and 3d shapes
8, Measurement - Length, Weight, Capacity, Learn about measurement - length, weight, capacity
9, Time, Learn about time
