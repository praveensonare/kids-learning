Reception Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count and order numbers to 20
- Add and subtract within 10
- Understand number bonds
- Recognize and create patterns
- Describe 2D and 3D shapes

Chapters:

1, Numbers to 20, counting, number order, more, less, estimation, number line
2, Addition and Subtraction, adding, subtracting, number bonds, within 10, strategies
3, Shape and Space, shapes, 2D, 3D, properties, patterns, symmetry
4, Measurement Basics, length, height, weight, capacity, time, comparing
