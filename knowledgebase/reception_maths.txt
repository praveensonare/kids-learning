Reception Mathematics - Cambridge Primary Curriculum

Learning Objectives:
- Count and order numbers to 20
- Add and subtract within 10
- Understand number bonds
- Recognize and create patterns
- Describe 2D and 3D shapes

Chapters:

chapter no: 1
name: Numbers to 20
description: Counting, Number order, More, Less

chapter no: 2
name: Addition and Subtraction
description: Adding, Subtracting, Number bonds, Within 10

chapter no: 3
name: Shape and Space
description: Shapes, 2D, 3D, Properties

chapter no: 4
name: Measurement Basics
description: Length, Height, Weight, Capacity
