# Reception - Maths Knowledge Base

## Cambridge Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) / Primary Stage 1

### Learning Objectives
- Count reliably to 20
- Recognize and order numbers 1-20
- Add and subtract within 10
- Recognize 2D and 3D shapes
- Understand measurement concepts

### Key Topics

#### Number
- Counting to 20 and beyond
- Number recognition and formation
- One more, one less
- Comparing numbers (greater/less than)
- Number bonds to 10

#### Addition and Subtraction
- Combining groups
- Taking away
- Finding the difference
- Number stories
- Using concrete objects

#### Shape and Space
- 2D shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle, pentagon, hexagon)
- 3D shapes (cube, sphere, cone, cylinder, pyramid)
- Patterns
- Position (behind, next to, in front)

#### Measurement
- Length and height (tall/short, long/short)
- Weight (heavy/light)
- Capacity (full/empty, more/less)
- Time (days of week, before/after, morning/afternoon)

### Activities
- Counting games and songs
- Practical measuring
- Shape sorting and building
- Number line activities
- Money recognition (1p, 2p, 5p, 10p)
